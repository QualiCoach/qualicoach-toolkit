Tool 08 — Structure

Literature Gap Finder

Get a preliminary read on where your topic may sit relative to the existing literature — population, knowledge, methodological, practical, and contextual gaps, each flagged as a starting point, not a finding.

Academic Integrity Notice: This tool generates preliminary, AI-assisted observations to help you begin exploring potential research gaps. It does not replace a systematic literature review, and it never fabricates citations, statistics, or study findings. All gaps must be verified against current peer-reviewed literature before use in a dissertation proposal.

Tool 09 — Structure

Literature Matrix

Turn a stack of article abstracts into a structured comparison matrix — purpose, methodology, findings, and how each one relates to your own study, side by side.

Academic Integrity Notice: This tool summarizes only what is stated in the abstracts you paste in — it does not access external databases and cannot verify sources independently. An APA citation is generated only when the pasted text itself contains enough bibliographic detail (authors, year, title, journal); otherwise the tool will say so rather than invent one. You are responsible for verifying every citation, finding, and detail against the original source before using this in your literature review.
