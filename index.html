QualiCoach Research Tools

Structure → Alignment → Clarity

Three guided builders to move your dissertation proposal forward — one piece at a time.

Tool 01 — Structure

Research Question Builder

Enter your study's core elements. You'll get a central research question, aligned subquestions, and a note on why each one fits your methodology — not a generic list, but questions built from your actual study.

Tool 02 — Alignment

Purpose Statement Builder

A purpose statement only works if every clause maps back to your actual study. Enter the four elements below and you'll get a formal statement plus a breakdown of exactly where each element shows up.

Tool 03 — Clarity

Interview Question Generator

Paste your research questions and methodology, and get a complete semi-structured protocol — opening, core, probes, and closing — ready to bring into your IRB application or pilot interview.
